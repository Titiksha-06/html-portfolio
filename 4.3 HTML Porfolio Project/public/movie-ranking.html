The Best Movies According to Titiksha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

I want to Eat your pancreas

It is a powerful reminder of the importance of cherishing the people in our lives and finding meaning in the smallest moments. It's a film that will resonate deeply with anyone who has ever struggled with loss, love, or the inevitability of life’s transient nature. Heartfelt, melancholic, and ultimately uplifting,this film is a must-watch for those who appreciate stories that speak to the heart.

Suzume

is a beautiful meditation on the resilience of the human spirit and the importance of embracing both the joys and sorrows of life. It’s a film that will leave you reflecting on your own experiences with love, loss, and the people who shape your journey. With its breathtaking animation, heartfelt story, and memorable characters, "Suzume" is a must-watch for fans of Shinkai's work and anyone who appreciates deeply emotional, visually striking films.

Weathering With You

It is a visually captivating, emotionally poignant, and ultimately uplifting experience that will resonate with anyone who has ever faced difficult decisions, pursued a dream, or fallen in love. It is a perfect example of how animation can be a powerful medium for telling stories that touch the heart and leave a lasting emotional impact.
